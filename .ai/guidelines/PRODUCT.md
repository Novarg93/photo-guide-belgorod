# Photo Guide Belgorod – Product Definition

## Vision

Create a structured photo session guide for Belgorod residents.

The website helps users:
- Understand what type of photo session they want
- Explore locations and studios
- Estimate budget
- Generate a structured brief to send to photographers

This is NOT a marketplace in phase 1.
This is a decision-support product.

---

## Target Audience

Primary:
- Couples planning weddings
- Families planning seasonal photo sessions

Secondary:
- People unsure what type of shoot they want

---

## Core Problem

Users:
- Don’t know what type of photo session fits them
- Don’t know good locations
- Don’t understand pricing
- Struggle to explain what they want

---

## MVP Features

1. Photo session categories
2. Location catalog (filtered by season)
3. Studio catalog
4. Photographer catalog (static)
5. Budget calculator
6. Brief constructor with shareable link
7. SEO guides (10–20 articles)

---

## What Makes This a Product

The Brief Generator.

User selects:
- Category
- Season
- Location
- Mood
- Clothing style
- Budget

System generates:
- Structured result
- Public shareable link
- “Send this to photographer” CTA

---

## Success Metrics (First 3–6 Months)

- 1000+ monthly visitors
- 20+ briefs generated
- Clicks to photographer pages
- Organic traffic growth

---
